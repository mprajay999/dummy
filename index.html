Our Story

Founded in 2020, Tsūki Izakaya brings authentic Japanese pub culture to Columbus. Our chefs combine traditional techniques with local ingredients to create dishes that honor both heritage and innovation.

We believe in the art of shared dining - small plates meant to be enjoyed with good company and premium sake. Our space blends minimalist design with warm, welcoming ambiance.
