Our Story

Driven by a passion for sharing South India's culinary heritage, our family opened Spice Route Columbus to bring authentic flavors and warm hospitality to our community. We blend traditional recipes with modern twists, creating a unique dining experience that celebrates both cultures.

Every dish tells a story - from the aromatic spices of Kerala to the comforting textures of American classics. Join us on this flavorful journey where tradition meets innovation.
