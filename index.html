Our Story

At Ruchi Indian Kitchen, we bring the rich and vibrant flavors of India to the heart of your community. Established in 2010, Ruchi was born out of a passion for authentic Indian cooking by Ruchi Patel. We aim to offer authentic dishes and take you on a culinary journey through India with every bite.
