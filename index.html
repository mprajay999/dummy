Our Story

Welcome to Raaga Indian Kitchen, where tradition meets taste!

Nestled in the heart of Tampa, FL, we bring the authentic flavors of India to your table. Founded with a passion for culinary excellence, our aim is to deliver a dining experience that's both authentic and innovative. Our skilled chefs use the finest ingredients to craft dishes that are rich in taste and steeped in tradition, providing you with a little piece of India right here in Florida.

With warm hospitality and a welcoming atmosphere, Raaga Indian Kitchen is your go-to destination for exquisite Indian dining. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, we're committed to making your experience memorable.
