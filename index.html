Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ruchi Indian Kitchen brings generations of traditional recipes to New York City. Our chefs use authentic cooking techniques and premium ingredients to create dishes that transport you straight to the heart of India.

We take pride in preserving culinary heritage while maintaining the highest standards of quality and freshness. Every spice is carefully sourced, and every dish tells a story of cultural richness and passion for flavor.
