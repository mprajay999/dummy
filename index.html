Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Alejandro Torres, Casa de Oro brings generations of family recipes to Columbus. We combine traditional Mexican cooking techniques with locally-sourced Ohio ingredients to create a truly unique dining experience.

Every dish tells a story - from our handmade tortillas to our complex mole sauces simmered for hours. We invite you to join us on this culinary journey through Mexico's diverse regions.
