About Us

At Ruchi Indian Kitchen, we bring the rich and vibrant flavors of India to the heart of your community. Established in 2010, Ruchi was born out of a passion for authentic Indian cooking, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our founder, Ruchi Patel, envisioned a place where locals could experience the warmth of Indian hospitality and the exquisite variety of spices and flavors that define Indian cuisine.
